## Understanding Carbon Data Integrity

Modern carbon accounting leaves digital traces that can reveal important information about emissions patterns. Our AI systems analyze:

- **Real-time energy consumption across facilities**
- **Supply chain emissions from Scope 3 sources**
- **Transportation and logistics carbon footprint**
- **Manufacturing process emissions and inefficiencies**

These carbon intelligence signals provide objective, continuous monitoring capabilities that comply with GHG Protocol, ISO 14064, and Science Based Targets initiative (SBTi) standards. Unlike spreadsheet-based tracking, our platform detects changes in real-time and identifies reduction opportunities automatically.

## Blockchain-Verified Carbon Credits

Carbon offset markets have faced credibility challenges due to double-counting, fraud, and lack of transparency. Our approach prioritizes immutability and auditability at every stage. Organizations maintain complete control over their data, with granular permissions for different stakeholder access.

We've implemented blockchain verification to ensure carbon credit authenticity. Instead of relying on manual audits alone, our technology creates cryptographic proof that carbon reductions actually occurred, when they occurred, and who should receive credit.
